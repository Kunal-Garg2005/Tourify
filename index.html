About India

India, the land of diversity, is known for its historical landmarks, diverse cuisine, and warm hospitality. From the Himalayas to the Indian Ocean, the Desert of Thar in the snowy mountains of Sikkim, it is a country full of beautiful landscapes and beautiful people. India is a unique country with diversity. "Unity is diversity" is the main slogan of the country
